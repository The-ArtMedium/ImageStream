How It Works

Point FocusCheck at any folder. It scores every image automatically and sorts them into three groups.

✅
Sharp

Keepers. In focus, ready to use. Copied into FocusCheck_Results/sharp/

🔧
Fixable

Soft but salvageable. Worth sharpening in LocalRAW or LocalShot. Copied into fixable/

🗑️
Rejected

Too soft to rescue. Copied into rejected/ so you can review before deleting.

What FocusCheck Does

⚡
Instant Scoring

Scores every image using Laplacian variance — the same method used by professional focus detection systems. Runs in seconds.

🖼️
Visual Preview

See thumbnails of each category before you delete anything. The GUI shows you exactly what is sharp, fixable and rejected.

🛡️
Non-Destructive

FocusCheck never deletes or modifies your originals. Everything is copied. You decide what to remove.

📊
Summary Report

After each scan you get a count per category, the single sharpest image and the softest image in the folder.

📁
Any Format

Works with JPG, PNG, TIFF and RAW files. Drop in a folder straight off the card — no conversion needed first.

✈️
Works in the Field

Runs on any laptop, even old hardware. No internet, no account, no cloud. Built for the field — from stadium to stable.
