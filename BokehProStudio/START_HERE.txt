CREATIVE vs EDITORIAL - AT A GLANCE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CREATIVE EDITION:
✓ Portrait photography
✓ Headshots and artistic work
✓ Click on eyes/face, blur outward
✓ Full artistic control
✗ Not ideal for sports/action where entire subject needs sharp

EDITORIAL EDITION:
✓ Equestrian photography (horse + rider sharp, arena blurred)
✓ Sports photography (player sharp, crowd blurred)
✓ Event photography (clean backgrounds)
✓ Maintains scene perspective
✗ Not ideal when you want radial artistic blur
