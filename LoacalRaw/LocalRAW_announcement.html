What It Does

Professional tools.
Zero compromise.

01
RAW File Support
CR2, NEF, ARW, DNG — Canon, Nikon, Sony, all major cameras. Plus JPG and PNG. Open any image, no conversion needed.
02
White Balance
LAB colorspace temperature and tint shifting. Not a crude RGB multiply — a perceptually accurate shift that looks natural on any image.
03
Exposure & Contrast
Real stops-based exposure. S-curve contrast applied to luminance only — shadows deepen, highlights open, midtones stay clean.
04
Noise Reduction
Dual-channel: luminance NL-means denoising removes grain, color channel smoothing kills chroma noise. High-ISO images become usable.
05
Luminance Sharpening
Unsharp mask on the L channel only. Detail comes forward without introducing color fringing or amplifying noise in the color channels.
06
Dehaze
Dark channel prior algorithm removes atmospheric haze and restores depth. Wildlife, landscape, and outdoor photographers know what this means.
07
Batch Rename
Rename entire folders using patterns: {prefix}_{date}_{n}. Live preview before any file is touched. Confirm and done.
08
Watermark
Text watermark with position control, opacity, and automatic shadow for readability on any background — light or dark.
09
Metadata Editor
Read full EXIF — camera, lens, ISO, shutter, aperture, date. Write Artist, Copyright, and Description. Your name stays with your image.
10
Live Preview
Debounced real-time preview on a downscaled working copy. Every slider feels instant. Full resolution is only processed at export.
11
Export Control
JPEG at 95% quality or lossless PNG. Full resolution. Your file, your format, your folder. Nothing uploaded anywhere.
12
5 Languages
English, Spanish, Portuguese, Arabic, Hindi. Over 3.5 billion people can use this in their own language on day one.
