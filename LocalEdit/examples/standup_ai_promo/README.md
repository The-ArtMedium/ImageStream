# StandUp AI Promo - Example Project

This is an example LocalEdit project showing how to create a promotional video for StandUp AI comedy content.

## What This Project Creates

A 30-second promotional video featuring:
- StandUp AI branded background
- Episode title and show time
- Comedy track audio from Suno
- Professional look ready for social media

## Assets Needed

Place these files in this directory:

1. **background.png** (1920x1080)
   - Your StandUp AI poster or branded image
   - Can be a solid color, gradient, or full design

2. **logo.png** (transparent PNG)
   - Your logo or branding
   - Recommended size: 200x200 to 400x400
   - Must have transparent background

3. **audio_track.mp3**
   - Your Suno-generated comedy track
   - 30 seconds duration
   - Keep it under 5MB for best performance

## How to Use This Example

### Method 1: Manual Recreation

1. Open LocalEdit
2. Click "Add Video/Image" → Select `background.png`
3. Click "Add Overlay" → Select `logo.png`
4. Click "Add Text" → Add your episode info
5. Click "Add Audio" → Select `audio_track.mp3`
6. Click "Export Video" → Save as `standup_promo.mp4`
