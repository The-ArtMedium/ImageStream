## What is LocalBeat?

A simple, accessible audio preparation tool.
Clip sounds, layer them, import from free libraries,
generate basic tones and rhythms — then export
directly to LocalEdit.

## Who is it for?

- 👴👵 Seniors discovering creativity
- 🎓 Students with no budget
- 🌍 Remote communities with limited hardware
- 👁 People with visual impairment
- 🎧 Anyone who thought audio was not for them
