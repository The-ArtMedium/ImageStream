# 🎨 Bokeh Studio Pro - Creative Edition

## What This Is

The **Creative Edition** gives you artistic control over depth of field with **radial point-based blur**. Click anywhere, and blur radiates outward from that point.

Perfect for portraits, headshots, and artistic photography where you want precise control over what's sharp.

---

## How to Use

1. Open `bokeh_studio_creative.html` in any web browser
2. Upload your photo
3. Click on the exact point you want sharp (e.g., model's eyes)
4. Blur radiates outward from that point
5. Adjust intensity and range to taste
6. Download your enhanced photo

## Pro Tips

### For Portraits:
1. **Click directly on the nearest eye**
2. Use **85mm f/1.4** or **Sony GM** preset
3. Blur intensity: **20-30**
4. Focus range: **120-180**
5. Enable **highlight boost** for eye catchlights

### For Product Photography:
1. Click on product's main focal point
2. Use **50mm f/1.8** preset
3. Lower blur (**15-20**) for subtle effect
4. Add slight vignette (**20-30%**)

### For Social Media:
1. Use **Sony GM** or **STF** preset for maximum impact
2. Higher blur intensity (**30-40**)
3. Enable **highlight boost**
4. Try **octagonal bokeh** for unique look

---

## Creative vs Editorial

**Use Creative Edition when:**
- You want to choose exactly what's sharp
- Portrait/headshot photography
- Product photography
- Artistic control over blur placement

**Use Editorial Edition when:**
- Sports/action photography
- Equestrian photography
- Entire subject needs to stay sharp
- Realistic lens simulation needed
