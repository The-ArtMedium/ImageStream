## Quick Distribution Methods

### Method 1: Email Distribution
1. Zip this entire folder
2. Upload to Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer
3. Share the download link in your club newsletter/email
4. Members download, unzip, and open `pro_bokeh_studio.html`

### Method 2: Club Website
1. Upload the entire package to your website
2. Create a download page
3. Link from your resources section
4. No special hosting needed - it's all static files

### Method 3: Social Media
1. Zip the package
2. Upload to cloud storage
3. Share the link on Facebook groups, Instagram, WhatsApp groups
4. Include the description below

### Method 4: In-Person Workshops
1. Bring the package on a USB drive
2. Let members copy it directly
3. Do a live demo showing how it works
4. Great for monthly meetings!

## Workshop/Training Session Outline

If you want to demo this to your club:

### 15-Minute Quick Demo

**Minutes 0-3: Introduction**
- Show before/after examples
- Explain what bokeh is and why it matters
- Mention it's completely free

**Minutes 3-8: Live Demo**
- Open the tool in browser
- Upload a sample photo
- Show different lens presets
- Demonstrate focus point selection
- Show before/after slider

**Minutes 8-12: Practical Tips**
- When to use which preset
- Settings for different photography types
- Common mistakes to avoid

**Minutes 12-15: Q&A and Distribution**
- Answer questions
- Share download link/USB
- Show them the START_HERE.txt file

### Sample Photos for Demo

Best demo photos have:
- Clear subject in foreground
- Busy or distracting background
- Some bright highlights (to show bokeh ball enhancement)

Good examples:
- Portrait against brick wall
- Horse and rider at show (busy arena background)
- Event photo with cluttered background
- Street photography with city lights

## FAQ for Club Leaders

**Q: Is this really free to share?**
A: Yes! MIT license. Share unlimited copies with zero restrictions.

**Q: Do members need to install anything?**
A: No. Just open the HTML file in any web browser.

**Q: Will it work on phones/tablets?**
A: Yes! Works on any device with a modern browser.

**Q: What if members have questions?**
A: The package includes complete documentation. They can also share 
questions in the club group.

**Q: Can we modify it for our needs?**
A: Absolutely! It's open source. If someone in your club codes, they 
can customize it.

**Q: Should we credit anyone?**
A: Not required, but nice to mention "Created by Claude" if you want.

**Q: Can we charge for workshops teaching this?**
A: The tool must stay free, but you can charge for your teaching time 
and expertise. Just don't charge for the tool itself.
